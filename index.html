Module 1  ·  Week 1
UI/UX Fundamentals & Design Thinking
+
1. What is UI vs UX — and why both matter
2. Design thinking: empathize, define, ideate, prototype, test
3. Visual hierarchy, spacing, and alignment principles
4. Typography for UI — choosing and pairing fonts
5. Color theory and building a UI color palette
6. Your first real-world design brief
Module 2  ·  Week 2
Figma Mastery — From Beginner to Pro
+
1. Figma interface, shortcuts & professional workflow
2. Auto-layout, constraints & responsive frames
3. Components, variants & reusable elements
4. Prototyping & interactive flows
5. Figma Variables & design tokens
6. Handoff to developers — inspect & export
Module 3  ·  Week 3–4
Design Systems & Component Libraries
+
1. What is a design system and why it matters
2. Building a component library from scratch
3. Atomic design methodology
4. Documentation & design system governance
5. Real project: build a full design system
Module 4  ·  Week 5
Portfolio Building That Gets You Hired
+
1. What clients and employers actually look for
2. Case study writing — telling your design story
3. Structuring your portfolio for maximum impact
4. Behance vs personal site vs PDF portfolio
5. Portfolio review session (live class only)
Module 5  ·  Week 6
Getting Your First Freelance Clients
+
1. Positioning yourself as a specialist, not a generalist
2. LinkedIn & Upwork profile optimization
3. Outreach scripts that actually get replies
4. Pricing your work — never undersell again
5. Proposals, contracts & client onboarding
Module 6  ·  Week 7–8
Career Strategy & Long-Term Growth
+
1. Job hunting: remote vs local vs agency vs startup

3. Salary negotiation for designers
4. Building your personal brand as a designer
5. Staying relevant — AI tools, trends, continuous learning
6. Final project presentation & graduation
